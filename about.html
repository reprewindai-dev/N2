About ShortFormFactory

ShortFormFactory is a done-for-you short-form video editing service built for agencies, consultants, and professional teams who need consistent weekly output without adding headcount. We handle post-production so your team can focus on strategy, clients, and growth.

What We Do

We edit short-form video content for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, and LinkedIn. Our clients send us raw footage or long-form recordings, and we deliver polished, platform-ready clips, typically within 12 to 24 hours.

Every package includes transparent flat-rate pricing, clearly defined deliverables, and no hidden fees. Our add-on system lets you customize each order to your exact requirements.

Who We Work With

Our core clients are marketing agencies managing multiple accounts, consultants building thought leadership, and podcast teams repurposing episodes into short-form content. Whether you need five clips a week or fifty, we scale with your workload.

How We Work

We match our editing style to your brand guidelines and client requirements. Every project is handled by professional editors using industry-standard tools. We stay current with platform trends, algorithm shifts, and format best practices so your content stays competitive.

Revisions are included based on your package tier: Basic includes 0 revisions, Standard includes 1 revision, Premium includes 2 revisions. All revision requests must be within the original project scope and submitted within 7 days of delivery. Out-of-scope changes are quoted separately.

Why Clients Stay

We remove a recurring operational bottleneck. Instead of hiring, training, and managing in-house editors, our clients get reliable output at a predictable cost. The result is more content published, fewer missed deadlines, and no HR overhead.
